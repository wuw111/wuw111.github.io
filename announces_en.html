U-craft Management Regulations V1.6.7 (Effective from January 30, 2024)

1. Entering the server signifies your compliance with these regulations.

2. Server Rules:

  • No killing, arson, harassment, or looting.
  • No bombing or dismantling of others' facilities. Dismantling must be reported to the admin.
  • No spawn killing of newcomers unless PVP has been mutually agreed upon in advance.
  • Members are prohibited from using cheating software/illegal items without the owner's consent.
  • No poaching players or advertising other servers. Discussion of other servers is generally prohibited. You may discuss the following servers in U-craft groups: 花雨庭, 布吉岛, 梦世界, Hypixel, 2b2t, JunCraft (FGA).
  • No insulting others' relatives or leaking others' private information inside or outside the server. Violators will be permanently banned!
  • No impersonating server management (such as admin, partner, moderator, etc.). Intentional violators will be banned for one month (30 days) and unbanned after adjustment!
  • Upon entering the U-craft server, you have the right to place and break blocks in areas where the server allows, but you may not arbitrarily destroy others' buildings, machines, etc.
  • If the legitimate rights and interests of U-craft players are infringed upon, please report to U-craft officials to apply for compensation (game resources/currency, etc.).
  • If a cheating player promotes hacks, mods, or any other software or plugins that violate the Cybersecurity Law (such as "X-Ray", "Kill Aura", etc.), do not accept them. Once you accept, even if you do not use them and regardless of your explanation, we will publicly announce and blacklist you server-wide!
  • The server management has no obligation to compensate for any virtual material losses caused by player disputes, unless the player is a cheater. International Edition server players can apply for compensation if items are lost due to server clear or crash, with only one chance.
  • In U-craft, if someone engages in unauthorized advertising, it should be reported to the server owner for banning, as only partners may conduct appropriate advertising within the server.
  • Any form of unreasonable pestering of management is prohibited.

U-craft International Bedrock Edition Additional Rules

  • Zombie Piglin Farm Rules: A single zombie piglin farm machine must not exceed 2 slices, and no more than 3 slices may be running simultaneously. Otherwise, it must be reported and ensured not to be used when the number of players exceeds [8-n] (n refers to the number of farm slices), otherwise it will be considered meaningless high-frequency redstone. Farms with no more than 2 slices can run at any time, but nevertheless, you may not operate a zombie piglin farm when the server TPS is below 6.0.
  • Sand Duper Rules: The maximum allowed sand duper size is 1x6=6 sand positions. Exceeding this will result in a warning and suspension (as sand dupers severely impact server performance). Sand dupers are only allowed for sand duplication and are strictly prohibited for other purposes (such as item duplication, dragon egg duplication, anvil duplication). Violators will have gains confiscated and be penalized accordingly.
  • Other Redstone Rules: No meaningless high-frequency redstone (criteria: total redstone-related block count > 10). First violation results in a warning + 30-day ban from using related redstone blocks. Second violation results in a permanent ban. Retaliatory bans will result in IP ban.
